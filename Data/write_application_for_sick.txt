Here is a sample application for sick leave:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State, Pincode]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Designation]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State, Pincode]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I am writing to request a leave of absence from work due to health reasons. Unfortunately, I have fallen ill and am currently under medical care.

I am experiencing [briefly mention your symptoms or illness, e.g., fever, flu, stomach infection, etc.]. My doctor has advised me to take [number] days of rest to recover and regain my health.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. I will provide a medical certificate from my doctor if required.

I would like to request sick leave from [date] to [date]. I appreciate your understanding and approval in this matter.

If there is any urgent work that needs my attention during my absence, please let me know and I will make arrangements to complete it remotely.

Thank you for your support and consideration.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

Please note that you should customize this letter according to your needs and the company's policies. Also, be sure to keep a polite and professional tone throughout the letter.
